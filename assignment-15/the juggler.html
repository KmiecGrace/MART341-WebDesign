The Juggler

Leon, a 23 year old medical student, becomes enamored of the strangely exotic Eliante, following her around to social engagements and functions.
He boldly making his presence and feelings known to her upon departure from one such unspecified event, she bemusedly and capriciously accedes to inviting him home.
And from here, we enter the head shaking insanity that comprises the remainder of the book, and what passes for their “relationship” throughout.
