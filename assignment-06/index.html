Eggs in Purgatory

By: Grace Kmiec

Ingredients

  • 5 cloves of garlic, smashed
  • Salt
  • black pepper
  • 20 onouces of cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bunch of spinach
  • 1/2 cup of onion sliced
  • 1/2 cup of sweet peppers, sliced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of broth, either vegatable or chicken
  • 4 slices of country bread, best with sourdough

Directions:

  1. In a medium saucepan put in the brooth, cherry tomatoes, onions, garlic, and sweet peppers.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil and then turn the heat down to a simmer.
  3. As the dish is simmering, smash it. This is to squish the tomaatoes.
  4. Mix in the spinach.
  5. With a spoon, make wells to crack the eggs into.
  6. Cover the saucepan, with a lid, and let simmer till the eggs are poached.
  7. As the eggs are poaching, toast the bread to desired texture
  8. Once the toast is done, spoon the tomato and spinach sauce over the bread and then gently lay eggs on top
  9. Add salt and pepper to taste
