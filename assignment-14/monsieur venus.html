The Juggler

When the rich and well-connected Raoule de Venerande becomes enamored of Jacques Silvert, a poor young man who makes artificial flowers for a living, she turns him into her mistress and eventually into her wife.
Raoule's suitor, a cigar-smoking former hussar officer, becomes an accomplice in the complications that ensue.
All done inn order to escape the ennui and malaise of her tradition-bound upper class existence, she must subvert and transcend social class, gender roles, and sexual morality.
